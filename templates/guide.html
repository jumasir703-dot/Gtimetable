Quick-Start Guide

A walkthrough of the main steps to set up and use your school's timetable.

1Grades & Streams

Pick a grade from the dropdown to see its streams. Add a stream name (e.g. "Grade 10 East") and, if it applies, a pathway — Arts & Sports Science, STEM, or Social Sciences under the CBC senior school structure.

2Teachers

Choose a teacher from the dropdown to view or edit their details, or add a new teacher with their name and rank. This is also where you'll assign which subjects and streams each teacher handles.

3Timetable Builder

With grades, subjects, teachers, rooms, and your day structure all set up, use the builder to lay out lessons across the week — either by placing them yourself or letting the auto-scheduler fill the grid while respecting your scheduling rules.

4View / Export

Once a timetable is built, view it by stream, teacher, or room. From here you can export to CSV for a spreadsheet, or download a print-ready PDF to post on a notice board.

The full setup order, if you're starting from scratch

  1. School Settings — name, tagline, and which weekdays you teach
  2. Grades & Streams — confirm your grades and add each stream
  3. Subjects — list what's taught
  4. Teachers — add staff and what they teach
  5. Rooms — the physical spaces available
  6. Day Structure — your periods, times, and breaks
  7. Scheduling Rules — any constraints the builder should respect
  8. Timetable Builder — lay out the actual lessons
  9. View / Export — check it over and share it
